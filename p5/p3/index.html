Project 3

(Artist Statement) For project 3, we had to make a clock that showed the time of something other than a normal clock showing how the day passes. I created a clock showing the cycle of when my dog needs to used the bathroom. He goes out to use the bathroom at 9 AM, 12 PM, 3 PM, 6 PM, 9 PM, and 12 AM, so at those times, a poop illustration pops up for ten seconds. At 4 PM, the sky changes to a dark purple to represent night time. This lasts until 7 AM, and the sky becomes blue again. The different dogs represent different measurements of time. The bottom represents seconds. The middle dog represents minutes. The top dog represents the hours.
