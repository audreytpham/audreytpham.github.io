About Me

Audrey Pham is an artist studing digital media art at San Jose State Univeristy in California. Hobbies of hers include creating art, playing video games, spending time with friends and family, and playing with her dog, Osiris. She enjoys going out to grab food, boba, or coffee with her friends every week. However, when at home, she spends hours playing video games and chatting on discord. Back in Vietnam, her grandfather made a living selling paintings and handcrafted cellos, guitars, violins, and more. It is to her and her mother's belief that she has received her skills from him. She has continued to polish these skills and learn more techniques by taking a variety of art classes in and out of school. She hopes to one day work for a video game design company.
